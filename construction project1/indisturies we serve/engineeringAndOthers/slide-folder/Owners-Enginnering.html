      Owner's Engineering

      Engineering Procurement Fabrication Construction Automation O+M Field Services Environmental

      Stay in control of your project with a trusted owner’s engineering partner.


      As your OE and PMC partner, Audubon drives capital efficiency, ensures quality, and upholds contractual integrity at every stage, reducing your risk and achieving your goals.


      From early planning through execution, our owner’s engineering (OE) and project management consultancy (PMC) services are built to support decision-makers navigating energy and infrastructure projects. We serve as an extension of your team, providing the experience, technical resources, and risk management expertise needed to keep your project on time, on budget, and aligned with your goals.


      We understand both the functional requirements of your project and the intricacies of project development. That means we focus on mitigating risk, safeguarding schedule certainty, and managing stakeholder and financial pressures—so that you can stay focused on your core business.

      Bringing structure to complex projects, our team adapts to your unique needs by integrating seamlessly with yours or taking the lead, always prioritizing safety, quality, and consistency.

      Minimize risk and maximize results with full-lifecycle owner’s engineering.
