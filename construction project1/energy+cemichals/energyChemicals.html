      Modernizing the World's Energy Infrastructure

      The energy and chemicals industries face growing demand, stricter regulations, and aging infrastructure. TeamBuilder delivers integrated, technology-driven solutions that help operators modernize assets and achieve long-lasting results.

      Oil and gas, pipelines, petrochemicals and refining, and LNG markets are facing pressure to operate smarter, cleaner, and more efficiently. At TeamBuilder, we tackle those challenges head-on with innovative engineering, procurement, construction, and field services.

      Whether developing greenfield infrastructure or upgrading existing facilities, our teams bring the expertise and flexibility required to improve performance, reduce risk, and increase efficiency for the entire life cycle of your assets.
